Certified Arborist

An arborist is trained in the art and science of caring for and maintaining trees. We keep one on the crew.

ISA Certified Arborist on staff

Our ISA Certified Arborist can diagnose a wide range of tree problems — fungus, disease, structural defects, decline — and then recommend the right response, whether that's treatment, pruning, or removal.

What an arborist visit gets you

  • A real diagnosis. We identify what's actually wrong instead of guessing, so you spend money on the problem and not the symptom.
  • Honest options. Sometimes the answer is a treatment plan; sometimes it's removal. We'll tell you which, and why.
  • A plan for the whole property. We look at the trees you keep, not just the one you called about, to head off the next issue.

Bringing in a certified arborist before you cut is the difference between saving a healthy tree and losing a salvageable one — or, just as often, removing a hazard before it removes itself onto your roof.

When to call the arborist

  • Leaves, bark, or limbs that look diseased or off-color
  • Mushrooms or fungus at the base or on the trunk
  • A tree that's declining and you don't know why
  • Before any major pruning or a removal decision
